# IFRS 15 — Revenue from Contracts with Customers (snippet for RAG demo)

**Standard:** IFRS 15

**Paragraph 35:** An entity shall recognize revenue when (or as) the entity satisfies a performance obligation by transferring a promised good or service to a customer.

**Paragraph 38:** For each performance obligation satisfied over time, an entity shall select an appropriate method to measure progress.
